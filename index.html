Welcome to DESPI

Desert Stream Partners International (DESPI) is an indigenous African missions agency committed to taking the gospel of Jesus Christ to unreached and underserved communities across Africa and beyond. We exist to transform lives, disciple nations, and glorify God in regions filled with spiritual dryness and humanitarian need.

“Behold, I will do a new thing… I will even make a way in the wilderness and rivers in the desert.” — Isaiah 43:19–20

Who We Are

DESPI is a faith-based, Bible-rooted missions organization called to proclaim Christ, disciple believers, empower communities, and plant sustainable churches. Our mandate is to bring the life-giving message of salvation to the unreached, beginning in Nigeria and Sudan and extending to the nations.

Core Values

  • God-Centered Living: Reflecting Christ through holy living and discipleship.
  • Passion for God & People: Showing compassion through spiritual and physical ministry.
  • Church Planting: Strengthening and expanding churches through training and partnership.
  • Interpersonal Respect: Embracing diversity with love, encouragement, and unity.
  • Servant Leadership: Training leaders through biblical, people-centered approaches.
  • Innovation in Missions: Using relevant, creative strategies to reach modern communities.

Statement of Faith

  • God: One God in three Persons—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus Christ: His deity, virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, resurrection, and return.
  • The Holy Spirit: Indwelling, empowering believers for holy living and service.
  • The Bible: The inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God.
  • Salvation: By grace through faith in Christ alone.
  • Humanity: All people are fallen and in need of redemption.
  • The Church: The unified Body of Christ, called to maintain purity and truth.
  • Resurrection: Eternal life for the saved; eternal separation for the lost.
  • The Great Commission: The Church is commanded to disciple all nations.

Mission & Objectives

Mission

  • Promote spiritual growth, discipleship, and socio-economic development.
  • Empower African communities for self-reliance and transformation.
  • Mobilize and equip the African Church for mission work and evangelism.

Aims & Objectives

  • Proclaim Christ across African cultures through holistic ministry.
  • Promote rural development and sustainability.
  • Strengthen church growth through training and discipleship.
  • Support vulnerable communities through empowerment and rehabilitation.
  • Partner with churches and mission bodies toward world evangelization.
  • Raise mission awareness, train, and send indigenous missionaries.
  • Support agricultural development and afforestation programs.
  • Develop leaders through capacity-building initiatives.
  • Plant self-sustaining indigenous churches.
  • Provide vocational training for women and youth.

Programs & Activities

Ministry & Leadership Training Program (MLTP)

A capacity-building initiative designed to develop Christian leadership and spiritual maturity.

Mission Exposure & Jesus Lifestyle Training (MEJET)

Teaches biblical foundations, mission strategies, and discipleship through heart, perspective, and action.

Strategic Discipleship Training

Develops character, spiritual formation, and ministry skills for new believers and community leaders.

Literacy Evangelism Training (LET)

Empowers individuals with literacy skills, promoting education and poverty alleviation.

Daughters of Destiny (DOD)

A mercy ministry reaching commercial sex workers, vulnerable children, and individuals with HIV/AIDS.

Mobilization & Awareness

Equipping churches to reach unreached people groups through workshops and training.

Community Development & Humanitarian Projects

Grassroots transformation through health, livelihood, and disaster relief initiatives.

Education Programs

Providing formal and informal education, skills training, and scholarships to uplift communities.

Counseling & Mentoring

Offering spiritual and emotional support, trauma care, and mentorship.

The Carmel Approach

A culturally relevant evangelism method using familiar community concepts.

The Treasure Project

Distributing solar-powered Arabic scripture devices across 20+ nations.

Food & Relief Ministry

Providing food, shelter, water, and medical help during crises.
