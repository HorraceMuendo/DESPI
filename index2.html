Who We Are

Bringing Living Water to Spiritual Deserts

Desert Stream Partners International (DESPI) is a faith-rooted African mission agency devoted to spreading the gospel to unreached peoples, especially within the Muslim world and remote African regions. We believe deeply in Christ’s command to make disciples of all nations and commit ourselves wholly to community transformation, church planting, and holistic outreach.

Inspired by Isaiah 43:19-20, we are called to make a way in the wilderness and rivers in the desert, bringing the life-giving water of Christ to those in spiritual dryness.

Our Core Values

God-Centered Living

Reflect Christ through discipleship and holy living. Our lives are testimonies that captivate others to turn from wickedness.

Passion for God & People

Demonstrate compassion through spiritual and physical ministry, ensuring the Great Commission doesn't become the Great Omission.

Church Planting & Growth

Partner with local churches for leadership development and evangelism, establishing self-sustaining congregations.

Respect & Relationship

Honor every individual's background with love, acceptance, and unity, helping each person attain full potential in Christ.

Servant Leadership

Focus on people, not projects — develop leaders biblically through training and workshops.

Innovative Strategy

Use relevant, creative means to reach modern communities, making the gospel accessible to every unreached person.

Statement of Faith

God

We believe in one God; the creator of all things eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, divine miracles, atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension, and personal return.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in God the Holy Spirit, who indwells all believers, enabling them to live holy lives and to witness for Christ.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the fully and uniquely inspired Word of God, infallible and sufficient for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe in salvation of the lost through regeneration by the Holy Spirit. Salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone.

Humanity

We believe all people are created in God's image but fallen in sin, unable to save themselves from God's wrath.

The Church

We believe in the spiritual unity of all true believers in Christ who comprise the Church - the Body of Christ.

Resurrection

We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost - eternal life for believers and eternal separation from God for the lost.

The Great Commission

We believe Christ commanded the Church to make disciples of all nations, baptizing and teaching obedience to all He commanded.

Our Mission & Objectives

Our Mission

  • Promote spiritual growth, biblical discipleship, and socio-economic empowerment in Africa.
  • Empower African communities for self-reliance, dignity, and sustainable development.
  • Mobilize and equip the African Church for outreach, discipleship, and missionary work.

Aims & Objectives

  • Proclaim the gospel across African cultures through holistic ministry.
  • Promote rural development and community sustainability.
  • Strengthen church growth through discipleship and leadership training.
  • Support vulnerable communities and destitute individuals.
  • Partner with churches and mission organizations toward world evangelization.
  • Coordinate mission awareness, recruit, train and send indigenous missionaries.
  • Support agricultural development and environmental stewardship.
  • Build local leadership capacity through structured training.
  • Plant self-sustaining, self-propagating churches.
  • Provide vocational training and empower women and youth.

Programs & Activities

1 Ministry & Leadership Training (MLTP)

Capacity-building to equip Christian leaders for ministry and spiritual maturity through structured training programs.

2 Mission Exposure & Jesus Lifestyle Training (MEJET)

Teaching biblical mission, discipleship, and effective outreach methods through "Know, Do, Be" modeling.

3 Literacy Evangelism Training (LET)

Empowering individuals with literacy skills and providing community awareness (e.g., HIV/AIDS education).

4 Daughters of Destiny (DOD)

Mercy ministry reaching vulnerable women, children, and individuals in need of care and rehabilitation.

5 Mobilization & Awareness

Engaging churches through workshops, seminars, and training for mission outreach and evangelism.

6 Community Development & Humanitarian Projects

Grassroots outreach including health, livelihood, disaster relief, and community empowerment.

7 Education & Skills Training

Formal and informal education, vocational training, and scholarships to uplift communities.

8 Counseling & Mentoring

Spiritual and emotional support, trauma care, and mentorship for vulnerable individuals.

9 The Carmel Approach

Culturally relevant evangelism using familiar community contexts to share God's love.

10 The Treasure Project

Outreach to Arabic-speaking nations through scripture distribution via solar-powered media devices.

11 Food & Relief Ministry

Emergency relief including food distribution, water, shelter, and basic medical support for displaced persons.

12 Strategic Discipleship Training

Developing believers' character, faith, and ministry skills through targeted discipleship programs.
