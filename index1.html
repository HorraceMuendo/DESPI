Desert Stream Partners International (DESPI)

Bringing the living water of Christ to the spiritual deserts of Africa and beyond. We are an indigenous, faith-based mission dedicated to evangelism, discipleship, community development, and church-planting among the unreached.

Who We Are

Desert Stream Partners International (DESPI) is a faith-rooted African mission agency devoted to spreading the gospel to unreached peoples, especially within the Muslim world and remote African regions. We believe deeply in Christ’s command to make disciples of all nations and commit ourselves wholly to community transformation, church planting, and holistic outreach.

Core Values

  • God-Centered Living: Reflect Christ through discipleship and holy living.
  • Passion for God & People: Demonstrate compassion through spiritual and physical ministry.
  • Church Planting & Growth: Partner with local churches for leadership development and evangelism.
  • Respect & Relationship: Honor every individual’s background with love, acceptance, and unity.
  • Servant Leadership: Focus on people, not projects — develop leaders biblically.
  • Innovative Mission Strategy: Use relevant, creative means to reach modern communities.

Statement of Faith

  • God: One God in three Persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus Christ: His virgin birth, atoning death, resurrection, and return.
  • The Holy Spirit: Indwelling believers, enabling holy living and service.
  • The Bible: Inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God.
  • Salvation: By grace through faith in Christ alone.
  • Humanity: All people are fallen and need redemption.
  • The Church: The unified Body of Christ, called to truth and purity.
  • Resurrection: Eternal life for believers; eternal separation for the lost.
  • The Great Commission: Make disciples of all nations, baptizing and teaching obedience.

Mission & Objectives

Our Mission

  • Promote spiritual growth, biblical discipleship, and socio-economic empowerment in Africa.
  • Empower African communities for self-reliance, dignity, and sustainable development.
  • Mobilize and equip the African Church for outreach, discipleship, and missionary work.

Our Aims & Objectives

  • Proclaim the gospel across African cultures through holistic ministry.
  • Promote rural development and community sustainability.
  • Strengthen church growth through discipleship and leadership training.
  • Support vulnerable communities and destitute individuals.
  • Partner with churches and mission organizations toward world evangelization.
  • Coordinate mission awareness, recruit, train and send indigenous missionaries.
  • Support agricultural development and environmental stewardship.
  • Build local leadership capacity through structured training.
  • Plant self-sustaining, self-propagating churches.
  • Provide vocational training and empower women and youth.

Programs & Activities

Ministry & Leadership Training (MLTP)

Capacity-building to equip Christian leaders for ministry and spiritual maturity.

Mission Exposure & Jesus Lifestyle Training (MEJET)

Teaching biblical mission, discipleship, and effective outreach methods.

Strategic Discipleship Training

Developing believers’ character, faith, and ministry skills.

Literacy Evangelism Training (LET)

Empowering individuals with literacy and providing community awareness (e.g. HIV/AIDS education).

Daughters of Destiny (DOD)

Mercy ministry reaching vulnerable women, children, and individuals in need of care and rehabilitation.

Mobilization & Awareness

Engaging churches through workshops, seminars, and training for mission outreach.

Community Development & Humanitarian Projects

Grassroots outreach including health, livelihood, disaster relief, and community empowerment.

Education & Skills Training

Formal and informal education, vocational training, and scholarships to uplift communities.

Counseling & Mentoring

Spiritual and emotional support, trauma care, and mentorship for vulnerable individuals.

The Carmel Approach

Culturally relevant evangelism using familiar community contexts to share God’s love.

The Treasure Project

Outreach to Arabic-speaking nations through scripture distribution via solar-powered media devices.

Food & Relief Ministry

Emergency relief including food distribution, water, shelter, and basic medical support.
